originating from Guagua, Pampanga, is a minimalist yet indulgent take on the traditional Filipino dessert. Unlike the typical halo-halo, which features a multitude of ingredients, Razon's version focuses on just a few key components, allowing each flavor to shine. The dessert comprises sweetened caramelized bananas (saba), rich macapuno (coconut sport) strips, finely shaved ice, and a generous drizzle of evaporated milk. It's crowned with a slice of creamy leche flan, adding a luxurious touch to the ensemble. This simplicity not only sets Razon's halo-halo apart but also highlights the harmonious blend of textures and tastes, making it a beloved treat across the Philippines.
