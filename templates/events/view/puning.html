Puning Hot Spring

place Angeles, Pampanga

Description

Nestled at the foothills of Mt. Pinatubo in Porac, Pampanga, Puning Hot Spring offers a unique blend of adventure and relaxation. Visitors embark on an exhilarating 4x4 journey through rugged terrains and lahar landscapes to reach the resort, where they can immerse themselves in thermal pools with temperatures ranging from 40°C to 70°C. Managed by the local Aeta community, the resort also features therapeutic sand and mud treatments, providing a holistic wellness experience amidst nature's splendor.
