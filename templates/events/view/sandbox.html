Sand Box

place Porac, Pampanga

Description

SandBox at Alviera in Porac, Pampanga, is a premier outdoor adventure park offering a variety of thrilling activities suitable for all ages. Visitors can experience the Philippines' tallest giant swing and Asia's first roller coaster zip line, Avatar One. The park also features the Aerial Walk Challenge, a high rope course designed to test agility and balance, and the Adventure Tower for wall climbing, rappelling, and free fall. For those seeking more relaxed activities, SandBox provides a themed kiddie playground, mini-golf, and picnic areas, making it an ideal destination for family outings and group adventures.
